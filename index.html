Make in India projects

The Indian Army's budget for 2025-26 focuses on modernization and self-reliance with key allocations for:

  • Future Ready Combat Vehicles (FRCV)
  • Upgradation of T-72 and T-90 tanks
  • New generation small arms and night vision
  • Advanced air defense systems
  • Enhanced border infrastructure
